## What is this?

Rensai (連載) is a blog covering anime, manga, light novels, visual novels, seiyuu,
anime music, idol culture, cosplay, otaku history, and more. New articles go up daily.
A weekly news roundup covers the most significant developments in the anime landscape.

The name comes from the Japanese word for serialization. Like a manga running weekly in
a magazine, Rensai publishes on a regular schedule across a wide range of topics.

## What kind of articles?

Every piece takes a specific angle and defends a position. Some examples:

- A psychological portrait of a character and what their design reveals
- How a studio's internal collapse shaped every studio that came after it
- The cultural mechanics behind maid cafes, idol elections, or otaku districts
- Why a specific era of anime openings hit differently and can't be replicated
- An industry deep-dive into production committees, disc sales, or labor conditions

The coverage goes wide - anime and manga, but also seiyuu careers, idol group dynamics,
Akihabara history, fashion subcultures, vocaloid, figure collecting, and convention
culture.
