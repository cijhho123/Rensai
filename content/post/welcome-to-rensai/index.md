Welcome to **Rensai** -- your daily source for fresh perspectives on anime, manga, and otaku culture.

## What is Rensai?

The name "Rensai" comes from the Japanese word for serialization -- the ongoing, regular publication of manga chapters and stories. Just like your favorite manga series, Rensai delivers new content on a daily schedule.

Every day, you will find new AI-generated articles covering a wide range of topics:

- **Anime reviews and analysis** -- deep dives into both classic and currently airing series
- **Manga recommendations** -- hidden gems and popular titles worth your time
- **Character studies** -- exploring the motivations and arcs of memorable characters
- **Industry insights** -- what is happening in the world of anime and manga production
- **Otaku culture** -- from conventions to collectibles, exploring the broader fandom

## Why AI-generated?

Rensai is an experiment in using AI to produce thoughtful, daily content about the things we love in otaku culture. Each article is crafted to offer genuine insight and spark conversation among fans.
